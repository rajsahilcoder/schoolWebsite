ABOUT NPS INDIRANAGAR

National Public School, Indiranagar is a co-educational school, founded in 1982 under the chairmanship of Dr. K. P. Gopalkrishna. This is the third school set up by Dr. K. P. Gopalkrishna after NPS Rajajinagar and NPS Chennai.
